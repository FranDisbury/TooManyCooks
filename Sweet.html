Currant Bread (Bara Brith)

  • 1 mug of brown sugar
  • 1 mug of sultanas
  • 1 mug mixed fruit
  • 3oz butter
  • 4oz cherries
  • 1 tsp mixed spice
  • 1 mug of cold tea
  • 2 mugs of self raising flour
  1. Add all the ingredients together in a saucepan.
  2. Boil for 5 minutes, then let the mixture cool.
  3. Beat 2 eggs and add to the mixture.
  4. Add 2 mugs of sifted self raising flour.
  5. Bake for 2 hours at 150°C.

Welsh Cakes (Pice ar y Maen)

  • 1lb plain flour
  • 1tsp baking powder
  • 12oz butter
  • 4oz currants
  • A dash of milk to bind
  1. Mix all the ingredients together.
  2. Roll out and cut into round shapes.
  3. Bake on a griddle, until slightly browned.
  4. Serve, sprinkled with sugar.

Caerphilly Scones (Teisen Radell Caerffili)

  • 1½oz butter
  • 12oz plain flour
  • 3 tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • Pepper to taste
  • 3oz Caerphilly cheese
  • 2oz Parmesan cheese
  • ½ pint of milk
  1. Sift the flour, salt and baking powder together, then rub in the butter.
  2. Grate cheese finely, add to the flour with a pinch of salt.
  3. Mix well, and add enough milk to make the dough soft.
  4. Roll out half an inch thick, cut out into rounds, and place on a baking sheet.
  5. Bake in a hot oven for 15 to 20 minutes.
  6. Serve warm, with butter.
